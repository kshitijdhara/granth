# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone. All contributors are expected to adhere to the Contributor Covenant v2.1.

Key points
- Be respectful and considerate in your communications.
- Harassment, discrimination, and hateful speech will not be tolerated.
- Respect privacy and follow applicable laws.

Enforcement
If you witness or are subject to unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers. See `SECURITY.md` for report details.
